About Star Wars: The Clone Wars Movie

This is an animated action movie set in star wars, released in 2008. It stars the three main characters Anakin Skywalker, Ahsoka Tano and Obi-Wan Kenobi. This is a movie that starts as a pilot to the television series of the same name. The TV series then premiered on Cartoon Network two months after the film's release.

Director: Dave Filoni
