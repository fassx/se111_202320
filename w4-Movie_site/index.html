Home/About Star Wars: The Clone Wars Movie

This is an animated action movie set in star wars, released in 2008. It stars the three main characters Anakin Skywalker, Ahsoka Tano and Obi-Wan Kenobi. This is a movie that starts as a pilot to the television series of the same name. The TV series then premiered on Cartoon Network two months after the film's release. The movie is about Anakin receiving a new padowan, all the while stopping the CIS from doing their evil schemes.

Director: Dave Filoni

Company Credits: Lucasfilm

Release Date: August 16, 2008

Genres:Animation, Action, Adventure
